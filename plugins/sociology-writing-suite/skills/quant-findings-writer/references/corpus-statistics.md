## Cross-Cutting Patterns

1. ~70% of articles open with a table reference
2. Descriptive statistics appear in opening 1-3 paragraphs of ~75% of articles
3. Robustness checks appear in 65%+ of quantitative articles
4. Magnitude contextualization is nearly universal (85%+)
5. 55% include subgroup heterogeneity analysis
6. Supplemental material references in 40% of articles
7. "Mechanism" language appears in 50% of articles even without formal mediation
8. Explicit hypothesis testing in only ~15%
